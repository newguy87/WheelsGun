Honda CBR 600RR

The Honda CBR600RR is a lightweight supersport motorcycle designed for both track riding and street performance. It is known for its excellent handling and smooth engine. Many riders love the CBR600RR because it is fast but also easier to control than larger superbikes.

  • Engine: 599cc
  • Top Speed: 257km/h
  • Horsepower: 113hp
  • Type: Supersport
  • Manufacturer: Honda
