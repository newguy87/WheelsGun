Kawasaki Ninja H2

The Kawasaki Ninja H2 is one of the most powerful production motorcycles ever made. It uses a supercharged engine designed by Kawasaki’s aerospace division. The bike is famous for its incredible acceleration and futuristic design.

  • Engine: 998cc Supercharged
  • Top Speed: 337km/h
  • Horsepower: 228hp
  • Type: Hyperbike
  • Manufacturer: Kawasaki
