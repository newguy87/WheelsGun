Ducati Panigale V4

The Ducati Panigale V4 is an Italian superbike inspired by Ducati’s MotoGP racing technology. It has a powerful V4 engine and advanced electronics that help riders control the bike at high speeds. The Panigale V4 is known for its aggressive design and incredible performance.

  • Engine: 1103cc V4
  • Top Speed: 299km/h
  • Horsepower: 214hp
  • Type: Superbike
  • Manufacturer: Ducati
